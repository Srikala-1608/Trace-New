Comprehensive Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Our Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) services provide a thorough examination of your digital ecosystem. From web applications and APIs to networks, servers, and mobile apps, we identify hidden weaknesses that could be exploited by attackers and offer actionable insights to close security gaps.

Using a blend of automated tools and advanced manual techniques, our ethical hackers simulate real-world cyberattacks to measure your organization’s resilience against known and unknown threats. This approach allows you to stay compliant, secure, and always one step ahead of cybercriminals.

Key Features of Our VAPT Services

Our VAPT methodology aligns with top industry frameworks, including OWASP, NIST, and ISO, ensuring a comprehensive review of your assets with clearly prioritized findings and in-depth recommendations tailored to your business environment.

  • In-depth vulnerability scanning using industry-leading tools.

  • Manual exploitation of vulnerabilities to determine actual risk.

  • Assessment reports aligned with OWASP Top 10 and CIS benchmarks.

  • Business impact analysis with CVSS scoring and threat modeling.

  • Remediation steps with step-by-step technical guidance.

  • Follow-up verification testing to validate fixes post-remediation.

What is Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT)?

VAPT is a security testing process that identifies, evaluates, and addresses vulnerabilities in IT systems, networks, and applications.

It includes both automated vulnerability scanning and manual penetration testing to simulate real-world cyberattacks.

Why does my organization need VAPT services?

VAPT helps identify weaknesses in your digital infrastructure before hackers do. It’s essential for safeguarding data, maintaining customer trust, and achieving regulatory compliance.

Regular assessments reduce the risk of data breaches and help you maintain a strong security posture.

Is VAPT required for compliance and certifications?

Yes, many regulatory frameworks such as ISO 27001, PCI DSS, and GDPR recommend or mandate regular VAPT assessments.

Our services are aligned with these standards, ensuring you meet audit and compliance requirements effectively.

What do I receive after a VAPT engagement?

You’ll receive a detailed report outlining identified vulnerabilities, severity levels, impacted assets, and step-by-step remediation guidance.

We also offer a retesting phase to verify that all critical issues have been effectively addressed.
