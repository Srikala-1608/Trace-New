Secure & Scalable Cloud Security Solutions

Trace offers robust cloud security services designed to protect your digital assets across AWS, Azure, GCP, and hybrid environments. As cloud adoption accelerates, so does the sophistication of cyber threats. We ensure your workloads are protected by embedding security from infrastructure to application layers.

From secure cloud architecture and identity management to threat detection, compliance, and incident response—we provide complete visibility and control over your cloud security posture. Our team ensures continuous protection, zero trust implementation, and automated risk mitigation to keep your organization safe and compliant.

Key Features of Our Cloud Security Services

We provide comprehensive solutions that integrate seamlessly into your DevOps lifecycle, offer deep security telemetry, and ensure you're always audit-ready. Whether migrating to the cloud or securing multi-cloud workloads, we tailor protections that scale with your growth.

  • Cloud Workload Protection Platforms (CWPP) for VM, container & serverless workloads.

  • Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) with real-time misconfiguration alerts.

  • Advanced Identity and Access Management (IAM) controls and zero trust enforcement.

  • Comprehensive visibility into multi-cloud assets, threats, and user activity.

  • SIEM and SOAR integrations for automated alerting and remediation.

  • DevSecOps alignment with CI/CD pipelines for secure application delivery.

Why Choose Trace for Cloud Security?

Trace combines years of security expertise with certified cloud architects to help you achieve resilience, scalability, and compliance in every step of your cloud journey. We design security-first strategies that minimize risks, reduce cloud costs, and streamline governance.

  • Certified Cloud Security Experts

    Our professionals hold credentials in AWS Security, Azure Security Engineer, and GCP Cloud Security certifications.

  • Compliance-Ready Frameworks

    Built-in controls and audit-ready reports to meet ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, and SOC 2 standards.

  • Proactive Threat Management

  • End-to-End Cloud Security

    From initial architecture and migration to ongoing runtime protection, we've got you covered.

What is cloud security?

Cloud security refers to the set of technologies, policies, and controls that protect data, applications, and services hosted in the cloud.

It ensures confidentiality, integrity, and availability across cloud platforms.

Why is cloud security important?

Cloud environments are often exposed to external threats. Strong cloud security prevents data breaches, misconfigurations, and unauthorized access.

It’s critical for regulatory compliance and business continuity.

Do you support multi-cloud security?

Yes. We offer multi-cloud security services for AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, ensuring consistent protection and visibility across your entire cloud ecosystem.

Our platform-agnostic approach ensures flexibility and control.

Is cloud security compliance guaranteed?

We help organizations meet cloud compliance by aligning controls with regulatory frameworks like ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and NIST.

We also assist with audits and continuous compliance monitoring.
